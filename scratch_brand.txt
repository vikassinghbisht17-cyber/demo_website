Tender Data Lake Pipeline
Government procurement portals publish tenders across hundreds of fragmented websites. Passageway’s distributed scraping fleet captures, cleans, and structures this data into a tiered data lake — delivering targeted tender intelligence to subscribers at scale.


What is it?
A worker-based, queue-driven architecture that scrapes tender data from 200+ government procurement portals, ingests documents with content-addressed deduplication, and lands structured data in a Bronze–Silver–Gold data lake.

Powered by RabbitMQ, MinIO, Postgres, and Redis. Designed to scale from 30K to 750K tenders per day without redesign.
PIPELINE STAGES

Scraping → listing & detail parsing

Document fetching → text extraction

Validation & reconciliation


Distributed Scraper Fleet
7 specialised workers connected by durable queues. Session management via Redis with epoch-based invalidation. Per-site rate limiting and CAPTCHA handling.


Bronze–Silver–Gold Lake
Raw captures preserved immutably. Cleaned, deduplicated data in Parquet/Iceberg. Star schema models for analytics and subscriber APIs.


Adapter Registry
YAML-driven site configs so adding a portal needs no code deploy. Fixture-based regression testing and canary promotions.
Scaling from 200 to 5,000+ procurement portals

Customer Propensity Intelligence
The most advanced enterprises don't react to customer behaviour. They predict it. Passageway's Customer Propensity Intelligence platform gives sales and CX teams the power to act before the customer decides.


What is it?
An ensemble ML scoring platform that processes historical customer behaviour, transaction data, product usage patterns, and external signals — assigning each customer a propensity score (0–100) quantifying their likelihood to take a specific commercial action within a defined time window.

Powered by XGBoost, Random Forest, and Logistic Regression. Validated against real conversion outcomes. Deployed at enterprise scale.
INPUT SIGNALS

Transaction history & recency

Product usage & service logs

Demographic & lifecycle segmentation


Purchase Propensity
Score customers likely to buy within 30/60/90 days. Right offer, right customer, right time.


Churn Propensity
Identify at-risk customers before they lapse. Trigger retention before churn — not after.


Upsell / Cross-sell Propensity
Predict readiness to upgrade based on lifecycle stage and usage trajectory.
Typical lift: 2–4× higher conversion vs. non-scored outreach
